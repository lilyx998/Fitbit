Fitbit

Health and Fitness

The Fitbit allows us to track our activity, exercise, food, weight, and more, for real-time information about our day.

We can workout however we like with workout modes that show stats like heart rate and calorie burn. We can even use Fitbit Coach to follow personalized video workouts that adapt to us and change over time.

Features

  • All-day Activity - Your Fitbit can track steps, heart rate, distance, calories burned, floors climbed, active minutes, hourly activity & stationary time. Lily & Abby love this feature, especially as they look back at their activity after band camp!
  • 24/7 Heart Rate Tracking - Get continuous heart rate tracking, follow trends over time & see real-time heart rate zones.
  • On-Screen Workouts - Get on-screen workouts that play on your wrist and coach you through each move.
  • Female Health - Use your watch with the Fitbit app to log your period, record symptoms & compare your cycle against other health stats like sleep, activity and weight.
  • The list goes on and on!
